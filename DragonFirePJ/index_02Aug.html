Dragon Boating

Dragon Boating

is a dynamic and exciting team building activity for anyone of any age or ability. Rapidly growing in popularity worldwide, this fun and motivating sport relies on everyone in the boat to work together toward a common goal,

turning Individuals into Teams.

DragonFire is a team building company specializing in Dragon Boat team building events for corporate clients.



What is Dragon Boating?

For those unfamiliar with the sport, Dragon Boating simply put, is a boat of 20 paddlers, a drummer and a steers person paddling to cross the finish line faster than their competition.


It's a fun, safe and inclusive activity ideal for clients looking for new and unique team building ideas.


The Corporate Connection to Dragon Boating

The corporate world shares many similarities with Dragon Boating. Working together is the key to success in both arenas. Both rely on cooperation, communication and commitment to achieve high levels of productivity.


The dragon boat experience fosters a strong sense of camaraderie and accomplishment among participants. Best of all, everyone comes away enthusiastic and energized with a renewed sense of common purpose. Dragon Boating epitomizes the very essence of working as a team.

The Team
"'Love what you do, and do what you love!"

And so, the idea for DragonFire was born.

With twenty years combined experience, we pride ourselves in training hard, pushing the limits & together we, ‘race to win.’

As long term members of a highly competitive Dragon Boat Team, a healthy dose of competitiveness & our passion for Dragon Boating has helped claim a number of significant titles both locally & internationally, including the UAE Women’s Club Championship 2014 and a 3rd place win at the Hong Kong International Dragon Boat Races in 2013.

We draw on our expertise & knowledge to motivate our Corporate Clients through our Team Building Events.

The opportunity for an exciting challenge, coupled with a strong sense of teamwork enables participants to experience success & pride both in the boat & back in the workplace.
