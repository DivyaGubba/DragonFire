Dragon Boating

Dragon Boating

DragonFire is a Dubai based teambuilding company specializing in dragon boating and outdoor teambuilding events for corporate clients.

Based at the Sheraton Jumeirah Beach Resort, we offer engaging and memorable teambuilding programs in a beautiful outdoor setting.

At the heart of our events is dragon boating, our signature teambuilding activity. This unique and exciting watersport is an ideal way to get all members of your team “onboard” working together towards a common goal.

For those who prefer to stay closer to the shore, you can enjoy our other teambuilding programs under the sunny skies; our Beach Olympics Tournament, our Treasure Hunt or Build-a-Boat Challenge.

,

As well as being great fun, our teambuilding programs are designed with key business outcomes in mind; to develop stronger teams through energizing, motivating and inspiring activities that focus on communication, cooperation and problem solving skills. Whether you are looking to do just one activity in a few hours, or a combination of activities over several hours or even a full day, we will design a program to suit your specific requirements.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Our philosophy at DragonFire is simple; we combine our teambuilding expertise with our passion for what we do to deliver memorable and meaningful experiences that exceed our client’s expectations.

We believe strongly in meticulous planning and attention to detail so that all aspects of your event run smoothly and successfully.

And most importantly, we aim to ensure that each individual comes away feeling engaged, energized and more a part of their team than when they arrived.


OUR TEAM

OUR TEAM

"'Love what you do, and do what you love!" -And so, the idea for DragonFire was born.

With over 20 years combined experience in both event management and dragon boating we pride ourselves in delivering professional and well organized events from conception to completion.

Trish Waterman, Managing Partner

Trish's varied career as a Public Relations executive has taken her from her native South Africa to London and finally Dubai where she settled with her family in 2008. As Managing Partner of DragonFire, she continues to capitalize on her excellent managerial, organizational & communication skills to positively enhance corporate relationships through the team building experience.

Trish has a long history in dragon boating, having competed both locally and internationally in the sport and successfully Captaining a women’s team at the Hong Kong International Dragon Boat Races 2013, alongside many other local victories. She currently paddles with, and coaches, a women’s competitive Dragon Boat Team in Dubai.

Melissa O'Brien, Managing Partner

Melissa is a longtime events professional having owned and operated two successful event entertainment companies in Dubai prior to the formation of DragonFire in 2014. Melissa brings a wealth of experience & knowledge of the Dubai market to the business. Her attention to detail & ability to effectively oversee each event from conception to completion ensures the smooth running of our teambuilding events. Melissa is passionate about the sport of dragon boating and has seen first hand the excellent teambuilding benefits for Dragonfire’s clients.

Melissa paddles with, and coaches, a Dubai-based women’s competitive dragon boat team and competes both locally and internationally in the sport.

TeamBuilding

DragonFire will work with you to ensure the right teambuilding program for your corporate event. We provide customized programs to suit each client’s requirements that can vary in length and include one or several of our teambuilding activities.

DragonFire’s onsite facilitation team will ensure a professional, well organized and seamless teambuilding event. Additionally, we will coordinate with the Sheraton’s food and beverage department on your behalf, or put you directly in touch with the banqueting team.
